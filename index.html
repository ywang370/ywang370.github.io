Short Biography

I obtained my PhD from Arizona State Unviersity in 2018, My advisor is Prof. Baoxin Li. I am interested in solving real world problems using computer vision and machine learning. At Adobe, I work on research related to deep learning and tech transferred several projects to Adobe Products.

Before joined to Adobe, I am lucky to have opportunities to work with Huan Liu (Arizona State), Jiliang Tang (Michigan State), Guojun Qi (Central Florida), Zhaowen Wang (Adobe Research), Zhe Lin (Adobe Research), Xin Lu (Adobe Research), Xiaohui Shen (Adobe Research), Yi Chang Yahoo Research), Neil O'Hare (Yahoo Research), and Liangda Li (Yahoo Research).
